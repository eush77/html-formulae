Syntax conventions:

  • indices and powers: “x^2”, “x_2”;
  • special symbols: “<=>”, “*”, “->”;
  • whitespace sequences: “␣␣␣”, “␣␣”, “\␣” denote (in order) em-space, en-space, and thin-space characters;
  • any correct HTML, including tags and entities.

Notes:

  1. “⇔” and “=” preserve appropriate spacing on both sides;
  2. “{}”-groupings in indices and powers are also available;
  3. “\” is the escape character, except when it occurs before the space character in “\␣”.
